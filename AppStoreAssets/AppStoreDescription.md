## Description

DisciplineX is the ultimate 90-day fitness transformation app built for people who are serious about changing their lives. Track daily disciplines, follow elite workout programs, and connect with a community of like-minded warriors.

DAILY DISCIPLINE TRACKING
- 7 daily tasks: workout, run a mile, hydrate, clean diet, no alcohol, personal growth, and progress photo
- Visual 90-day calendar with completion tracking
- Pass system for rest days
- Daily notes for journaling your journey

ELITE WORKOUT PROGRAMS
- 4 complete programs: Urban Inferno (HIIT), Zenith Strength (powerlifting + mindfulness), Rhythm & Flow (movement mastery), Iron Will Powerlifting (competition prep)
- Every workout details specific exercises with sets, reps, and rest periods
- Programs progress through multiple phases from foundation to peak performance
- Add your entire program to iPhone Calendar or Google Calendar with one tap

FULL WORKOUT LOGGER
- Log custom workouts with exercises, sets, reps, and weight
- Browse exercises by muscle group and category
- Track workout history and total volume
- View personal bests for every exercise

BODY STATS & PROGRESS PHOTOS
- Log weight, body fat, measurements
- Visual weight trend charts
- Front, side, and back progress photos
- Photo history timeline

COMMUNITY
- Share progress, tips, recipes, and stories
- Join goal-based and regional groups
- Direct and group messaging
- Support forum for help and motivation

Built for discipline. Built for results.
